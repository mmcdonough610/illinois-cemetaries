Land Cover Context of Illinois Cemeteries

Map made by: Megan McDonough

This map was made using QGIS 4.0.0. It has two images, one of the entire state of Illinois, and the second is focused on the Northeastern part of the state. It shows every Illinois cemetery and its associated land cover type. These types are Agriculture, Developed, Forest, Other, Shrub/Grass, Water, and Wetland. This was accomplished by filtering the attribute tables through the Query Builder, for both the NLCD layer and the cemetery layer. To get the Illinois outlines it was 'Name' = "Illinois", whereas for the cemeteries it was 'FEATURE_NA'="Cemetery". Once filtered, connecting the two layers was next. This was done through a longer connection in the Query Builder. These were a series of "When and Then" statements connecting values in the NLCD layer to the cemetery layer. After the Query was completed, the new label layer was changed to a Categorized color system, making the cemetery layer different colors for what land cover classification they were.
